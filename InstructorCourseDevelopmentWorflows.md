# Instructor Workflows for Course Development
This document outlines the various workflows that instructors can follow to effectively develop and manage their courses. Each workflow is designed to streamline the course creation process, enhance student engagement, and ensure a high-quality learning experience.
## 1. Course Planning Workflow
### Steps:
1. Identify courses to teach
1. Define course objectives and learning outcomes.
1. Associate objectives with courses.
1. Create a course outline and syllabus.
1. Select appropriate teaching methods and materials.
1. Review and finalize the course plan.
